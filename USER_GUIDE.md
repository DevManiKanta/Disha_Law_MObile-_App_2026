# DISHA - Law Firm CRM App User Guide

## Welcome to DISHA! 👋

DISHA is a simple app to manage your law firm's clients, appointments, and follow-ups. Let's learn how to use it!

---

## 📱 Getting Started

### Login
1. Open the app
2. Enter your **email** and **password**
3. Tap **"Sign In"** button
4. You're in! 🎉

---

## 🏠 Main Navigation (3 Tabs)

The app has **3 main sections** in the sidebar:

### 1. **Home** 🏠
- See your daily overview
- View total calls, clients, and appointments
- Quick action buttons to add clients or appointments
- See recent activity

### 2. **Clients** 👥
- View all your clients
- Search for specific clients
- Filter by date and lead type (Hot, Warm, Cold)
- Click on any client to see full details

### 3. **Follow-ups** 💬
- See all follow-up messages
- Filter by date
- Quick actions to call, message, or schedule

---

## 👥 Managing Clients

### View All Clients
1. Tap **"Clients"** in the sidebar
2. See list of all clients
3. Use filters to find specific clients

### Add a New Client
1. Go to **Home** tab
2. Tap the **blue "+" button** in the middle of the bottom
3. Fill in the form:
   - **Call Type**: Incoming or Outgoing
   - **Lead Type**: Cold, Warm, or Hot
   - **Full Name**: Client's name
   - **Phone**: Contact number
   - **Location**: City/Area
   - **Reference**: How you got this client
   - **Case Type**: Type of legal case
   - **Remarks**: Additional notes
4. Tap **"Save Client"** button

### View Client Details
1. Go to **Clients** tab
2. Tap on any client name
3. See complete information:
   - Contact details (phone, email, location)
   - Case information
   - Activity stats
   - Remarks

### Call a Client
1. Open client details
2. Tap the **red "Call Now"** button at the top
3. Or tap the **phone number** in contact section
4. Your phone dialer will open

### Send WhatsApp Message
1. Open client details
2. Tap the **green "WhatsApp"** button
3. WhatsApp will open with the client's number

### Send Email
1. Open client details
2. Tap the **orange "Email"** button
3. Your email app will open

---

## 📅 Managing Appointments

### View Appointments
1. Go to **Home** tab
2. See "Appointments Today" card
3. Or tap the **"Appointments"** tab in the bottom (inside Home)

### Schedule an Appointment
1. Go to **Home** tab
2. Tap the **blue "+" button**
3. Select **"Create Appointment"** from the client form
4. Or tap **"Schedule Appointment"** on client details page
5. Fill in:
   - **Appointment Type**: Online or Offline
   - **Client**: New or Existing
   - **Date**: Pick from calendar
   - **Time**: Select time
   - **Fee Amount**: Cost of appointment
   - **Payment Method**: Cash or Online
   - **Remarks**: Notes
6. Tap **"Save"**

---

## 💬 Managing Follow-ups

### Add a Follow-up
1. Open a **client's details** page
2. Tap **"Add Follow-up"** button
3. A popup will appear
4. Type your follow-up message
5. Tap **"Save"** button
6. Message is saved! ✅

### View All Follow-ups
1. Tap **"Follow-ups"** in the sidebar
2. See all follow-up messages with client names
3. Use filters: All, Today, This Week, Pending
4. Each follow-up shows:
   - Client name
   - Message
   - Date and time
   - Quick action buttons

### Quick Actions on Follow-ups
From any follow-up card, you can:
- **Call**: Tap to call the client
- **Message**: Tap to send a message
- **Schedule**: Tap to schedule an appointment

---

## 🔍 Filtering & Searching

### Filter Clients by Date
1. Go to **Clients** tab
2. Tap date filter buttons: Today, Yesterday, This Week, Custom
3. See only clients from that period

### Filter Clients by Lead Type
1. Go to **Clients** tab
2. Tap lead filter buttons: All, Cold, Warm, Hot
3. See only clients with that lead status

### Search Clients
1. Go to **Clients** tab
2. Use the search box at the top
3. Type client name
4. Results appear instantly

## 💡 Tips & Tricks

### Quick Tips:
1. **Color Coding**: 
   - 🔴 Red = Hot leads (urgent)
   - 🟠 Orange = Warm leads (interested)
   - 🔵 Blue = Cold leads (new)

2. **Save Time**: Use the **"+" button** to quickly add clients or appointments

3. **Stay Organized**: Add follow-up messages immediately after client calls

4. **Filter Smart**: Use date and lead filters to focus on important clients

5. **Call Directly**: Tap phone numbers to call instantly

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### App Won't Open?
- Restart your phone
- Reinstall the app
- Check internet connection

### Can't Call a Client?
- Make sure phone dialer is enabled
- Check phone permissions
- Try copying the number manually

### Follow-up Not Saving?
- Check internet connection
- Make sure message is not empty
- Try again

### Forgot Password?
- Tap "Forgot Password?" on login screen
- Follow email instructions
- Create new password
